A night of Jazz: San Francisco

Jeremy music performed a night of jazz at the ABC Club on July 18, 2023. The team consisted of David on piano, mo' dean on bass, and marvy on drums. They played a selection of original compositions and jazz standards for an enthusiastic audience of about 500 people.

The concert started with a lively piece called “XYZ Blues”, which showcased the trio’s tight coordination and improvisational skills. The piano played a catchy melody over a swinging rhythm section, while the bass and drums took turns soloing. The audience clapped along and cheered after each solo.

The next piece was a ballad called “My Romance”, which featured a beautiful piano introduction and a tender bass solo. The trio played with sensitivity and expression, creating a romantic mood. The audience listened attentively and applauded warmly at the end.

The third piece was a fast-paced bebop tune called “Donna Lee”, which challenged the trio’s technical abilities and speed. The piano played complex chords and lines over a driving bass and drum groove, while the bass and drums also displayed their virtuosity in their solos. The audience was impressed by the trio’s energy and skill.

The concert ended with a funky piece called “Watermelon Man”, which invited the audience to participate by singing along with the catchy riff. The trio played with groove and fun, making the audience dance and smile. The audience gave a standing ovation to the trio and asked for an encore.

Jeremy music delivered a night of jazz that was entertaining and engaging for both the performers and the audience. They demonstrated their musical talent and versatility in playing different styles and moods of jazz. They also communicated well with each other and with the audience, creating a positive atmosphere. The concert was a success and a memorable experience for everyone involved.
